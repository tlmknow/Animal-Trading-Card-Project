Tiger Shark

All Tiger Sharks do not have stripes.

  • Scientific Name: Galeocerdo cuvier
  • Average Length: 20 to 25 Feet
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Oceanic and Shallow Coastal waters

The Tiger Shark have stripes on the sides of their body. The stripes fade over time. It is considered the fourth largest shark.
